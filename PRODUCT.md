# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Dream Lux ERP is used by a high-end event management team in Addis Ababa, including the Owner/CEO, operations managers, accountants, event managers, and inventory officers. They work across HR, payroll, inventory, and event operations, often needing fast access to records during active business workflows.

## Product Purpose

The product centralizes HR records, payroll, inventory tracking, event lifecycle management, and operational reporting for Dream Lux PLC. Success means staff can find, update, and trust operational records quickly while role-based access keeps sensitive financial and employee data controlled.

## Brand Personality

Professional, polished, and calm. The interface should feel enterprise-grade and premium without becoming decorative, playful, or visually noisy.

## Anti-references

Avoid cartoonish widgets, sci-fi language, oversized novelty UI, generic SaaS hero-card patterns, and heavy decorative shadows. The system should not look like a game, a consumer social app, or a loosely themed AI-generated dashboard.

## Design Principles

- Put operational speed before ornament.
- Make records easy to scan, compare, and act on.
- Preserve a premium Dream Lux feel through restrained gold accents, clean contrast, and disciplined spacing.
- Keep bilingual English and Amharic workflows complete, not partial.
- Prefer stable, predictable enterprise controls over experimental interaction patterns.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard access for core workflows, respect reduced-motion preferences, and keep layouts usable down to 320px wide mobile screens.
