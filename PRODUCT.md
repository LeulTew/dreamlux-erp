# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Dream Lux ERP is used by high-end event-company operators: owners, managers, HR/payroll staff, inventory controllers, and event coordinators who need fast, trustworthy operational records during event planning, execution, reconciliation, and reporting.

## Product Purpose

The product centralizes Dream Lux operations across event management, HR/payroll, inventory, expenses, and reporting. Success means teams can create and price events, assign resources, reconcile inventory movement, pay staff accurately, and inspect profitability without fragmented spreadsheets or missing audit history.

## Brand Personality

Premium, disciplined, and calm. The interface should feel enterprise-grade for a luxury events company: polished and confident, never playful, cartoonish, game-like, or overly decorative.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS decoration, oversized marketing-page composition inside app screens, excessive shadows, inflated radii, sci-fi language, game terminology, cartoon widgets, and unclear low-contrast gray text. Do not use visual gimmicks that weaken data scanning or operational trust.

## Design Principles

- Keep workflows dense but legible so operators can scan lists, compare records, and act quickly.
- Preserve clear service boundaries between UI rendering, business rules, data access, and external integrations.
- Make financial, payroll, and inventory state explicit with audit-ready histories and reversible workflows.
- Prefer familiar enterprise controls over invented interaction patterns.
- Use Dream Lux's premium gold and slate/purple identity as restrained accents that support task clarity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast for text, icons, borders, focus states, and interactive feedback. Maintain responsive usability down to 320px, honor reduced-motion preferences, and keep hover/focus states visibly distinct.
